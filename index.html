Internet Infrastructure of Wellington

An observation at how the internet and information is distributed around Wellington CBD

Modems Cables Cameras Man Holes Power Lines

Modems and Repeaters

Access Point

A Wifi Access Point is a device that connects wireless devices together to form a wireless network. The Universities wifi network is connected across several buildings without the need for a cable.

Wireless Repeater

Here, the existing Wireless network is rebroadcasted through an additional device. Widening the broadcast area of the Wifi.

Cables

Inner Cabling

Wiring in homes and business carry electrical current to all utilities and outlets within the building.

Outdoor Cable

Within multiple buildings, cables are run throughout the walls and ceilings. These cables connect the building and its residents to a wider network of electricity and the internet.

Cables

These cables link up to a much wider network that will help connect the city through electricity.

Cameras

Personal Surveillance

A camera used as a security measure. Monitoring the residents as they use the common areas of the building. This feed is used for security purposes and is likely protected

CCTV

These cameras are situated outside of most larger businesses. Providing more information to ensure the security of the building. This feed may be accessed by the law enforcement if required.

Traffic Camera

A camera used by government to monitor traffic and pedestrians. This feed is among a wider network that is connected..

Man Holes

United Networks

One of the former owners of the Wellington Regions electricity network. Beneath this is an assortment of cables linked to the cities utilies.

WCC

Manholes are connected to all types of underground systems. These underground networks can allow us to send data to eachother efficiently, but also connect multiple different variants of networks, such as water.

Power Lines

Power Line

A core feature in transferring electricity to and from individuals homes and businesses. With this electricity also comes data and communication between one another.

Power Line

Recieving electricity into our spaces allows us to an array of communication types. Ranging from Wifi to Landline, helping us stay connected.
